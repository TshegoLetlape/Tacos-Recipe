What You'll Need: 2 Chicken Breasts, 2 Tortillas,Olive Oil, Grated Cheese, Diced Avocado, Chopped Tomato, Chopped Green Pepper, Chopped Onion, 1 tbsp Garlic Powder, 1 tbsp Chilli Powder, Salt & Pepper, Spicy Mayo
Instructions In 5 Easy Steps: Step 1: Dice up chicken breasts, season with spices and let chicken marinate for +30 minutes.
Step 2: Heat up a precious amount of olive oil in a saucepan and fry the chicken till it turns Golden Brown.
Step 3:Put the tortilla in a pan till mildly crispy.
Step 4: While the tortilla is still in the pan, top it up with the chicken and grated cheese of your choice till it melts.
Step 5: Remove the tortilla from the pan and top it with the diced tomato, onion & pepper, avocado and spicy mayo.
